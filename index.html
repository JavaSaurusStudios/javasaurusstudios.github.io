Who am I?

Hi, I’m Kenneth, but you might know me online as DrJavasaurus!

I am a Belgian software engineer and content creator. Since 2018, I've been building an online presence in software and game development, particularly in the Twitch scene. When talking to content creators and influencers in Flanders, odds are pretty high they'll have seen active me in their chat.

I am always looking for new challenges, so allow me to present myself through this website!

Professional Game Developer

For the last two years, I've worked as a researcher in game development. My main focus has been augmented and virtual reality and artificial intelligence. In 2024, I've joined SAY IT Labs, a growing ed-tech startup company, as a software engineer in voice recognition and speech technology.

But even in my free time is largely oriented in game development. I love participating in game jams and even am writing up a beginner's guide to game jams! (MORE NEWS COMING SOON)

Scientist, PhD and freelance programmer

I obtained a PhD in bioinformatics & life sciences back in 2018. I've specialized in proteomics and have kept my passion for science alive by working as a freelance bioinformatician in various pharmaceutical companies during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Part of being a scientist is communication, which I love integrating in my livestreams. When viewers have a question, I am more than happy to go out of my way to discuss and elaborate !
